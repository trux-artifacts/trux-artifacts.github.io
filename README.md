# TruX Artifacts

**[Visit the TruX Artifacts website](https://trux-artifacts.github.io)**

TruX Artifacts is the public catalog of research outputs from the
[Trustworthy Software Engineering (TruX)](https://www.uni.lu/snt-en/research-groups/trux/)
group at the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust (SnT),
University of Luxembourg.

The website brings together the concrete outputs released alongside our research so that
they are easy to discover, understand, reuse, and cite.

## What you will find

- **Datasets** for empirical software engineering, Android analysis, security, and
  multilingual natural language processing
- **Tools** for static and dynamic analysis, malware analysis, program repair, fuzzing,
  and LLM-assisted software engineering
- **Libraries** providing reusable building blocks for research and analysis pipelines
- **Benchmarks** supporting the evaluation and comparison of research approaches

Each artifact page explains the purpose of the research output and, when available,
provides its accompanying paper, a ready-to-copy BibTeX entry, and direct links to the
code, data, models, or other resources.

The collection covers research areas including Android datasets, Android security and
malware, static and dynamic analysis, patches and program repair, LLMs for software
engineering, multilingual NLP, networking, and fuzzing.

## Using the artifacts

The artifacts are shared with the research community to support reproducibility and new
research. If you use an artifact in your work, please cite the corresponding paper listed
on its page.

## Contact

Questions about a specific artifact are best directed to its authors, who are listed on
the artifact page. For questions about the catalog, please open an issue in this
repository.
